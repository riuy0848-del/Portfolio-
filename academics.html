Sheet 05 / How Programs Are Structured

From coursework to capstone

Every undergraduate program follows the same four-stage arc, regardless of school.

Year 1–2

Foundations

Core math, physics, and discipline fundamentals, taught alongside a required design-build studio.

Year 2–3

Specialization

Students choose a track and join a faculty-led lab as a research assistant, not just an observer.

Year 4

Capstone

A year-long project for a real client — a municipality, a company, or an internal Vertex lab.
